WARPATH

A turn-based invasion. Out-think and overrun the enemy army holding the east.

OBJECTIVE

Win by capturing the enemy HQ ⌂ (move Infantry/Recon onto it) or destroying their whole army. You lose if your army is wiped out or the enemy seizes your HQ.

HOW TO PLAY

1. Deploy — spend your budget to build a force in the west zone (left 3 columns). Click a unit, click a tile. Click a placed unit to refund it.
2. Battle — click a unit → blue = move range, red = targets. Click a tile to move, then click an enemy to attack. END TURN when done.
3. Reinforce — earn gold each turn from your HQ & captured cities. Hit BUILD to spend it on new units next to your HQ.

TIPS

Capture neutral cities ▣ for income & healing · units on your buildings heal each turn · forests/mountains reduce incoming damage · ranged units (Artillery) avoid counterattacks · kills earn veterancy ★ for bonus damage.

FIELD MANUAL

CONTROLS

Click your unit → click blue tile to move → click red enemy to attack. END TURN passes to the enemy. BUILD deploys reinforcements by your HQ. Press H to toggle this manual.

UNITS (rock-paper-scissors)

🪖 Infantrycheap, captures HQ/cities
🏍️ Reconfast scout, captures
🛡️ Tankcrushes infantry, weak to anti-tank
💥 Artilleryrange 2–3, no counterattack, fragile
🎯 Anti-Tankshreds tanks & helicopters
🚁 Helicopterflies over water/mountains, fast
➕ Medicheals an adjacent ally instead of attacking

TERRAIN

▣ City (capture for income/heal) · 🌲 Forest (−30% dmg) · ⛰ Mountain (blocks ground, −40% dmg) · ≋ Water (blocks ground; helicopters fly over) · ⌂ HQ (heals, capture to win)
